Beaches & Islands

Discover Cambodia's pristine tropical beaches and idyllic islands in the Gulf of Thailand

Sihanoukville

The gateway to Cambodia's beautiful coastline and island paradise.

Sihanoukville, also known as Kampong Som, is Cambodia's premier beach destination. Located on the Gulf of Thailand, this coastal city offers a mix of beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and easy access to nearby islands.

The city has several beaches to choose from, each with its own character. Ochheuteal Beach is popular with backpackers and offers a long stretch of sand with beach bars and restaurants. Serendipity Beach is known for its relaxed atmosphere and beautiful sunsets.

While Sihanoukville has seen rapid development in recent years, it remains the main departure point for boat trips to the stunning islands of Koh Rong and Koh Rong Samloem, where you'll find some of Southeast Asia's most beautiful beaches.

Tropical Island Paradise

Koh Rong

Koh Rong is the larger of the two main islands, known for its vibrant nightlife and beautiful beaches. Long Beach (Sok San Beach) stretches for 7 kilometers of pristine white sand and crystal-clear turquoise water.

The island offers a range of accommodations from budget bungalows to luxury resorts. Activities include snorkeling, diving, fishing, and jungle trekking. The island's main village has restaurants, bars, and shops, while other areas remain relatively undeveloped.

Highlights: Long Beach, snorkeling, bioluminescent plankton, sunset views

Koh Rong Samloem

Koh Rong Samloem is the quieter, more laid-back sister island of Koh Rong. This smaller island is perfect for those seeking peace and tranquility. The island has limited electricity and fewer amenities, making it ideal for a true escape.

Saracen Bay is the main beach, featuring powdery white sand and calm, clear waters perfect for swimming. The island is less developed than Koh Rong, with eco-friendly resorts and bungalows that blend with the natural environment.

Highlights: Saracen Bay, Lazy Beach, snorkeling, relaxation

Other Islands

Beyond Koh Rong and Koh Rong Samloem, Cambodia's coastline is dotted with smaller, less-visited islands. Koh Ta Kiev offers rustic beach camping and a true back-to-nature experience.

Koh Russey (Bamboo Island) is a private island with luxury resorts, while Koh Thmei is part of Ream National Park and offers pristine nature and wildlife viewing opportunities.

Highlights: Secluded beaches, wildlife, eco-tourism, pristine nature
